    • Dhaka

    Dhaka is the capital and the largest city of Bangladesh. It is the ninth-largest and sixth most densely populated city in the world, with a population of 8.9 million residents within the city limits and a population of over 21 million. Dhaka is the economic, political and cultural center of Bangladesh. It is one of the major cities of South Asia.

    Chittagong

    Chittagong, officially known as Chattogram and also known as the Port City of Bangladesh, is a major coastal city and financial centre in southeastern Bangladesh. The city has a population of more than 2.5 million while the metropolitan area had a population of 4,009,423 in 2011, making it the second-largest city in the country.
